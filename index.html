Is this for you?

This program is for moms (30–45), including postpartum, who want real strength and confidence — not another cycle of burnout and starting over.

This is for you if:

  • You’re tired of starting over every few months.
  • You want to feel strong and athletic — not just “smaller.”
  • You want coaching that supports your nervous system, not constant stress.
  • You want structure and accountability without rigid rules.
  • You want to build muscle and confidence, not obsess over the scale.

This is not for you if:

  • You want a quick fix, detox, or “30-day shred.”
  • You’re looking for a generic PDF plan with no coaching.
  • You’re not ready to commit to consistency and support.

How it works

You’ll get strong support up front, then we intentionally build independence — so you leave confident, capable, and self-led.

Phase 1: Supported

Weeks 1–6: structure, reassurance, education, form feedback, and consistency systems.

Phase 2: Empowered

Weeks 7–12: decision-making confidence, routines that stick, and fewer “what do I do?” moments.

Phase 3: Mentored

Weeks 13–16: identity shift, sustainability, and long-term strategy you can trust.
