FAQ

Common questions before you apply.

Do I need access to a full gym?

No. You can follow the program with a home gym or a commercial gym. Your plan is personalized to your equipment and schedule.

Is this postpartum-friendly?

Yes. Programming can be adjusted for postpartum considerations and your current training level. If you have medical restrictions, you’ll follow guidance from your healthcare provider.

Will I have to track macros?

No. You’ll get fueling guidance focused on building muscle and supporting your nervous system. If tracking helps you, it can be used strategically — not obsessively.

What if I’m starting from scratch?

You’re welcome here. The plan is built around your starting point and progresses at a pace that’s challenging but sustainable.

How do the weekly calls work?

You’ll have a weekly coaching call for support, education, and accountability. Details are shared after acceptance so times align with the group.

How soon can I start?

After you apply, you’ll be contacted with next steps. Start dates depend on current enrollment and cohort timing.
