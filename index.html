FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is this 1:1 coaching or group?

This is hybrid coaching: you get a personalized plan plus weekly coaching calls and check-ins. Support is designed to be high-touch early on, then shift into mentorship as your confidence grows.

Do I need a gym?

A gym is helpful, but not required. If you train at home, we’ll build your plan around the equipment you have and adjust as needed.

Is this postpartum friendly?

Yes. Programming can be adapted for postpartum needs and your current capacity. If you have medical concerns, we’ll stay within appropriate training scope and coordinate with your provider when needed.

Will I have to track calories or weigh myself?

No. This program is muscle-focused and structure-focused. We can use simple nutrition guidelines and habits without obsessing over the scale.

How soon will I see results?

Most clients notice improvements in energy, consistency, and confidence early on. Visible physique changes follow as strength and habits build over the 16 weeks.

What happens after 16 weeks?

You’ll leave with a sustainability plan and the skills to lead yourself. If you want ongoing mentorship, you’ll have options to continue.

What if I’m not accepted?

If it’s not the right fit (timing, readiness, needs), you’ll be notified. The goal is to protect your progress and ensure every client gets the support they deserve.
