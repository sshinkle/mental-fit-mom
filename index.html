FAQ

Is this program postpartum-friendly?

Yes. Programming is adjusted based on your current season, training history, and recovery needs. If you’re newly postpartum or have specific concerns, we’ll tailor your plan accordingly.

Do I need a gym?

A gym is helpful but not required. If you have home equipment, we can build an effective plan around it.

What if I’m overwhelmed and inconsistent?

That’s exactly who this was built for. The program starts with higher support and structure, then helps you build confidence and independence over time.

Will this focus on weight loss?

We focus on strength, muscle building, and body composition—not scale obsession. Many clients lose fat as a result of consistent training and supportive lifestyle changes.

How do the weekly calls work?

Weekly calls provide coaching, education, mindset support, and Q&A. They’re designed to keep you progressing without feeling alone.

What happens after I apply?

You’ll receive the next steps. If it’s a fit, we’ll confirm details and get you onboarded.
